My Approach

I consider my approach to counseling to be holistic. I like to look at all aspects of client functioning in designing a treatment plan that is most likely to be effective. I use EMDR to treat a variety of issues where trauma is considered a root cause, including PTSD, depression, anxiety, and related disorders. Lifestyle, stress management, diet, exercise are also considered. I believe strongly in the Family Systems approach in understanding the cause of the root issue being treated. I also use an integration of CBT (cognitive-behavioral therapy) and ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy â€“ a mindfulnes-based cognitive approach) in working with self-limiting beliefs and thought patterns. I am very interested in natural approaches to treating mental health issues and am constantly learning more about new interventions. I work to help clients handle relationship concerns and achieve balance in various areas of life. I am unashamedly a Christian, but fully respect the faith perspective of all of the clients I serve. My goal is to constantly be growing in my knowledge and expertise and to always be looking for the most effective treatment methods that will bring the fastest relief.
