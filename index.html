Welcome to my website! I hope you find the information and resources valuable!

Gregory Isch, MA, LMHC, NCC

My Training: I hold a Master’s Degree from Indiana Wesleyan University in Clinical Mental Health Counseling with a certificate in Addictions Counseling. I also hold a Master’s Degree from Liberty University in Counseling. Additionally, I have a Bachelor’s Degree from IPFW in Business Management and Marketing with a minor in Psychology. I have also received training in EMDR and am currently working toward EMDR certification with Dr. Barb Hensley (see http://www.cincinnatitraumaconnection.com/dr.-hensley.html ). I am a member of EMDRIA (EMDR International Association). I went into private practice and formed Isch Counseling Services, LLC and joined Dr. Michelle Miller, Psychologist, at Life Transitions Counseling Center in Fort Wayne in March, 2015 (see www.lifetransitionscounselingcenter.net ). Prior to this, I have counseled at a not-for-profit private practice, a public school system, and a local church.

MA = Master’s of Arts Degree

LMHC = Licensed Mental Health Counselor in the State of Indiana, License #39002613A

NCC = Nationally Certified Counselor by the National Board for Certified Counselors (NBCC)

About me: My personal passions include natural approaches to health and wellness, nutrition and food, and exercise. Prior to my counseling career, I worked in the banking industry for 11 years. I have been married to my high school sweetheart for 21 years, and we have 6 children. I enjoy spending time with my family, traveling, playing sports, and learning.
